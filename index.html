The Chronicles of Awakened

The year is 1889, but not as history remembers it. The Great Awakening of 1851 revealed that certain individuals could interface directly with the Aetheric Network - a dimension of pure potential that powers our steam-driven world.

These "Awakened" individuals can manipulate reality itself, bending steam, brass, and clockwork to their will. But with great power comes great danger, as the Aetheric energy threatens to consume those who cannot control it.

The Royal Society of Natural Philosophers seeks to understand this phenomenon, while the Church of the Eternal Gear views it as heresy. Meanwhile, the common folk whisper of mechanical men walking the streets and airships that sail between worlds.
